========================================
   NORMAL CONDITION DEMO
   (NO Priority Inversion)
========================================

SCENARIO:
----------
1. Low task acquires resource (holds for 5 sec)
2. High task needs same resource -> gets BLOCKED
3. NO medium task to interfere
4. Low task continues running (nothing preempts it)
5. Low task releases resource -> High task runs

  [HIGH] - Priority 2 (needs resource)
  [LOW]  - Priority 1 (holds resource)
